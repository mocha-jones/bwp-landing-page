About

Fight-week coverage built for people who actually watch the matchups.

Bloody Water Podcast is where UFC cards get broken down without the filler. Every episode is built around the same idea: what matters, what is being missed, and where the sharp value really sits.

The show moves through betting angles, stylistic reads, live underdogs, and the momentum around each card, while keeping the conversation direct and watchable for fans who care about the fights as much as the numbers.

The homepage stays intentionally light. The newest episode leads, the latest releases stay tucked behind a reveal, and the rest of the archive is there when you want to dig in.
